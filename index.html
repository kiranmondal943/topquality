Expert Insights

Do you offer custom glass cutting services?

Yes. We provide precision CNC glass cutting for a wide range of architectural and structural glass types. All cutting is executed according to exact project drawings and site measurements, ensuring accuracy, clean edges, and full compatibility with installation requirements for commercial, residential, and industrial projects.

Are your aluminum profiles and systems SASO certified?

Absolutely. All our aluminum profiles, façade systems, and structural components are fully compliant with Saudi Arabian Standards Organization (SASO) regulations. Our materials are selected and tested to meet stringent quality, safety, and durability standards suitable for the Kingdom’s environmental conditions.

Do you handle both fabrication and installation?

Yes. We offer end-to-end solutions, including design support, fabrication, supply, and professional on-site installation. This integrated approach ensures quality control, efficient timelines, and seamless coordination throughout the project lifecycle.

Can you deliver projects across Riyadh and nearby regions?

Yes. Our operations are based in Riyadh, and we regularly execute projects across the city and surrounding regions. Delivery timelines are planned to meet project schedules while maintaining strict quality and safety standards.

Do you provide technical support and after-sales service?

Yes. We remain fully committed even after project completion. Our team provides technical assistance, maintenance guidance, and post-installation support, ensuring long-term performance and client satisfaction.
